I am a Heilbronn Research Fellow in Mathematics at the University of Bristol.

Previously, I was a postdoc at NTNU working with Drew Heard as a member of the project tt-geometry in Trondheim. Before that, I was postdoc at UCLA working with Mike Hill.

My PhD thesis advisor at the University of Oregon was Dan Dugger.

My research is in algebraic topology, an area of pure/theoretical math. My work is mainly in equivariant homotopy theory with a particular interest in RO(G)-graded Bredon cohomology, structured ring spectra, parametrized cohomology, tensor triangular geometry, and representation theory.
