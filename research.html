Research

My research is in algebraic topology. I work in equivariant homotopy theory with a focus on RO(G)-graded Bredon cohomology, structured ring spectra, parametrized cohomology, tensor triangular geometry, and representation theory.

Click here for a brief introduction to RO(C2)-graded cohomology.
